Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026
🔒 Short version: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store or sell any personal information. The app works fully offline after the first load.

1. Data Storage

BOQ Quick Estimator stores all project data exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not use analytics or advertising pixels
  • We do not transmit your project data, client names, or quantities to any server
  • We do not require account registration to use the app

3. Access Codes

When you enter a Pro access code, the code is stored in your browser's localStorage only. It is not transmitted to a server. Code validation is performed locally in your browser.

4. Contact Information

If you contact us via WhatsApp or email to order a Pro plan, your contact details (name, phone, email) are used only to process your order and send your access code. This information is handled by Al Qaswaa Al Sharqia Trading EST and is not shared with third parties.

5. External Services

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) and a PDF library from cdnjs.cloudflare.com. These services may log your IP address as part of standard web server logging. Please review their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Deletion

To delete all saved project data, clear your browser's localStorage for this site. In most browsers: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files / Site data.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at info@qaswasharqia.com or via WhatsApp at +966 51 175 9092.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
